Who are we?

Travis Baker Coding Company is a privately run tech company dedicated to spreading information about knowledge surrounding the tech industry and meeting the tech needs of its clients.

Travis Baker founder and CEO of TBCC is a developer with a history of educating youth on a variety of topics ranging from swimming to coding.

It is the mission and passion of TBCC to provide excellence in the services that it provides. TBCC will help to build apps, webpages, games, etc.

Or individuals who wish to learn more about the tech industry, whether for a passion project or to start their coding careers, can learn all they need to know in a variety of languages used by the top companies in the industry.
